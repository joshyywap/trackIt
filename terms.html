Terms & Regulations

Welcome to TrackIt! Before using the system, please read the following terms and regulations. These guidelines help ensure a fair, organized, and responsible borrowing experience for everyone.

Authorized Users

Welcome to the TrackIt Borrowing and Return System. This website is intended solely for the authorized administrators, teachers, and Grade 12 – Dewey students. Login access is restricted to these authorized users only.

Students from other sections are welcome to browse and view the available laboratory items. However, borrowing requests must be made through an authorized Grade 12 – Dewey student.

Borrowing Responsibility

All borrowers are expected to handle laboratory equipment responsibly. Borrowed items must be returned on or before the agreed return date in the same condition in which they were received.

Any lost, damaged, or unreturned item may require the borrower to replace the item, pay its equivalent value, or comply with other disciplinary measures in accordance with the school's policies and laboratory regulations.

QR Code Policy

The QR code attached to every laboratory item is an essential part of the inventory system and must not be removed, covered, altered, or damaged.

Anyone found intentionally removing or damaging a QR code may be required to shoulder the cost of replacing the QR label, restore the item's identification, and may face appropriate disciplinary action as determined by the laboratory administrator or school authorities.

Feedback & Reports

We highly encourage all users to submit ratings, suggestions, and reports of any system errors, damaged equipment, missing items, or other concerns.

Your feedback is valuable and will help improve the TrackIt system and provide a better borrowing experience for future users.

Research Notice

This website has been developed exclusively for the pilot implementation of a research study. It is intended for academic, testing, and evaluation purposes only. Features, policies, and system functions may be updated or modified throughout the duration of the research.

User Agreement

By accessing or using TrackIt, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to comply with these Terms and Regulations. Your cooperation helps maintain an efficient, organized, and reliable laboratory borrowing system.
